Green Smoothie

Description

Perfect for hot days

Ingredients

  • 1 pineapple
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 kiwis
  • 200 ml orange juice
  • 1 handful spinach

Steps

  1. First clean the pineapple, cut out the stalk and puree the flesh with the orange juice.
  2. Now peel the bananas and kiwis, add them and puree them as well.
  3. If you have gotten young spinach, simply wash it, add it and mix it through, remove the stalks from large spinach leaves and only put the leaves in the blender.
